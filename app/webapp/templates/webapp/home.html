Why Open

The World's Most Comprehensive Open Sailing Resource

Built by sailors, for sailors. Our mission is to create the world's largest and most comprehensive open-access sailing data source, completely free from ads and paywalls.

Real-World Boat Data

Search across community-provided data about sailboats, including real-world measurements from boat owners, not just factory specs.

AI Boat Assistant

Get personalized boat recommendations from Salty, our AI sailing agent, to help you find your perfect vessel.

Personal Boat Logbook

Add your boat to our database and maintain a detailed logbook with trips, photos, events, and modifications.
